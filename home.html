About our program

Welcome to the GPA Pilot, where your ticket to academic success is just a click away! When using our platform, students can track and manage their academic progress in real-time. We also provide students with a comprehensive view of their academic progress and allow them to identify areas of improvement quickly and easily. This is the perfect way to stay on track and succeed academically!

We are students from Middleton High School and our mission is to empower individuals on their educational paths, GPA Pilot is more than just a GPA calculator; it's a useful base of tools designed to help navigate students through the world of all academics. We strive to provide the best resources that are all user-friendly.

Everyone can be a mentor

Mentorship is a collaborative relationship where an experienced individual, known as the mentor, offers guidance and support to help a less experienced person, the students, develop personally and professionally. Through open communication and shared experiences, mentors provide valuable insights and feedback, fostering a supportive learning environment. Ultimately, mentorship contributes to the growth and success of individuals as they navigate their journeys.
